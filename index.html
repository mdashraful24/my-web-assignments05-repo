Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

Bangladesh's 2024 quota reform movement was sparked by reinstating the 30% reservation for descendants of independence war veterans in the civil service. Protests erupted, led primarily by students and young professionals, demanding a reduction in quota percentages, prioritization of merit, and greater transparency. The movement intensified following a court ruling reinstating the quota, leading to mass demonstrations, internet shutdowns, and clashes with security forces.
